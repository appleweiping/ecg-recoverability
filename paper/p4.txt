5. CONCLUSION
Reduced-lead ECG reconstruction is not just ill-posed but illposed with a known physical structure. Exploiting the cardiac
dipole turns “trust the reconstruction” into a per-feature certificate: exact recovery where the dipole is spanned (with a
conditioning κs (S) that warns where it is not), a calibrated interval where the population constrains the residual, and a flag
where nothing does. It tells you, before any reconstruction,
which features a lead configuration can support—and shows
that fabrication is a choice of objective, not an accident of the
network. The certificate is reconstructor-agnostic and wraps
any method.

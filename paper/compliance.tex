\section*{Ethics, data, funding, and AI-use disclosure}
\noindent\textbf{Data and ethics.} All experiments use publicly available, fully de-identified
databases distributed under open licences: PTB-XL \cite{wagner2020ptbxl} and the
Chapman-Shaoxing-Ningbo 12-lead ECG database \cite{zheng2020chapman} (PhysioNet). No new
human-subjects data were collected; no identifiable information is used. This is a
methodological study and does not constitute clinical advice.

\noindent\textbf{Funding and competing interests.} No specific funding supported this work. The
author declares no competing financial or non-financial interests.

\noindent\textbf{Generative-AI use (per IEEE policy).} An AI coding assistant (Claude,
Anthropic) was used to help implement experiment code, orchestrate runs, and edit prose. It did
not originate the scientific claims; every numerical result is produced by the released,
lineage-stamped pipeline and was checked by the author, who takes full responsibility for the
content.
